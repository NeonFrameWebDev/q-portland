The Kitchen

Built around the farmers, not the other way around.

Victor Martinez grew up in Hidalgo, Mexico, farming corn, fava beans, and squash with his family. That's not a detail on a bio page. It's why the food at Q tastes the way it does.

Since opening in 2016, Chef Martinez and Executive Sous Chef Brianna Martinez have built a kitchen around scratch cooking and supplier relationships that go back years. Many of their purveyors were small startups when Q first started working with them.

The menu pulls from the Pacific Northwest, travels through the Americas, and keeps going. Eclectic is the right word. So is deliberate.

Whether you're stopping in for the lunch prix fixe or settling in for a tasting menu, you're welcome here.
